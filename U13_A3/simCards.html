SIM cards are small chips that go into your phone, SIM cards are for providing data like having your own wifi signal where ever you go. This is because they are small computer chips that connect to cell networks, which then allows you to make calls, send texts or use your own mobile data. SIM cards also contains information about you, like your phone number and your account details to allow it to connect to cellular networks and provide a connection. Every phone has a SIM card as it is important for using your phone abroad. SIM cards are not needed for connecting to home Wi-Fi or using external calling or messaging services like Whatsapp and Discord instead of the built in calls and messenger.

O2 is a SIM card service company they have cell towers accross the globe and you can purchase a subscription of a SIM card to gain access to their cell service. O2 is most prominent in the UK but you can buy plans that allow you to use your SIM card when abroad in Europe.
