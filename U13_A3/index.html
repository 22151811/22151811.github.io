SIM cards are small chips that go into your phone, SIM cards are for providing data like having your own wifi signal where ever you go. This is because they are small computer chips that connect to cell networks, which then allows you to make calls, send texts or use your own mobile data. SIM cards also contains information about you, like your phone number and your account details to allow it to connect to cellular networks and provide a connection.
